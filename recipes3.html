Light gratin dauphinois

Gratin dauphinois or pommes de terre à la dauphinoise is a traditional au gratin dish of French cuisine, originally from the Dauphiné region, made with potatoes and milk.

Strawberry Shortcake!

When strawberries start to appear, there's nothing better than a dessert to celebrate them! To do this, get out your boudoir cookies - it's time to make a charlotte!

Yogurt cake

Yogurt cake is a dessert based on plain yogurt. The pot of yoghurt is used to measure out the other ingredients, making it simple and accessible to children.

Cauliflower gratin

Gourmet cauliflower gratin is a comforting dish. To round it off, many cook it on a layer of minced meat. It's a good way to get a revisited shepherd's pie.

Chocolate mousse

Chocolate mousse is a dessert traditionally made with a minimum of chocolate and egg white, beaten until stiff. It can sometimes be embellished with egg yolk, sugar or whipped cream, spices or citrus zest.

Chili con carne

Chili with meat" is the literal translation of Chili Con Carne. It's a spicy stew served with vegetables such as tomato and onion, as well as beans and meat. Many prefer it served with rice.

Sand Rose

Want to make sand roses at home? The recipe is easy! Take some cereal, add some dark, white or milk chocolate melted in a bain-marie and a little fat (butter, margarine...) and you've got delicious bite-sized treats to munch on at coffee time.

Honey gingerbread

Halfway between bread and cake, the recipe for gingerbread is more than a thousand years old: its ancestor, honey bread, was already known to the Egyptians and Greeks in ancient times! Now associated with eastern France, this delicacy is based on a honey and spice (cinnamon, ginger, aniseed...) mixture baked in a cake tin. Cut into slices and enjoy as a snack, in a Flemish carbonnade or with foie gras!

Brownies

The brownie is a chocolate cake, melting in places, baked in the oven. A glaze can then be applied to its surface. The cream can also be eaten without being baked. The ingredients needed to make a brownie are sugar, eggs, flour, butter and chocolate. The best-known recipe is the one with pieces of walnut or hazelnut.
